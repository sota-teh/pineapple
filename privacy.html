Privacy

The short version: Pineapple keeps to itself. No accounts, no telemetry, no analytics — nothing about how you use it ever leaves your machine. The only thing it does over the network is check this site for a newer version.

Who we are

SOTA TEH d.o.o., Pod bukvami 24, SI-1000 Ljubljana, Slovenia (“we”) makes Pineapple and is the controller for the little data described here. Contact: pineapple [at] sota-teh [dot] com.

What Pineapple processes, and why

  • This website: a plain static page — no cookies, no analytics, no third-party scripts. It is served by GitHub Pages (GitHub, Inc.); like any web host, GitHub receives standard connection data such as your IP address in order to deliver the page.
  • The app on your machine: your settings live in a local file. Nothing is collected, profiled or sent anywhere — Pineapple has no analytics and no account to sign in to.
  • Checking for updates: the one exception. Pineapple fetches a small version file from this site (and, if you choose to update, the download package). That is an ordinary web request, so the host sees your IP address at that moment — exactly as when you open the page yourself — and no identifier or personal data is sent.
  • Email: if you write to us, we receive your address, name and whatever your message contains, and use it solely to reply (GDPR art. 6(1)(b) — steps prior to a contract; art. 6(1)(f) — legitimate interest in answering correspondence). Messages are kept as ordinary business records and shared with no one, except where the law requires it.

Your rights

We hold nothing about you beyond any email you send, so there is usually nothing to access or erase — but you may still request access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, or object to processing: one email to pineapple [at] sota-teh [dot] com is enough. You can also lodge a complaint with the Slovenian supervisory authority, the Information Commissioner (ip-rs.si).
